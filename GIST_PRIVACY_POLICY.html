Privacy Policy for Bojang - Learn Tibetan Language

Effective Date: December 2024

Last Updated: December 2024

Quick Summary: Bojang does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data stays on your device.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Bojang handles information when you use our mobile application Bojang - Learn Tibetan Language.

Information We DO NOT Collect

Bojang is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • User-generated content
  • Account information (no login required)
  • Payment information (app is free)
  • Contacts or photos

Information We DO Collect

Local Storage Only:

  • Quiz progress and scores (stored locally on your device only)
  • App preferences and settings (stored locally on your device only)

Important: This information never leaves your device.

How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Save your learning progress
  • Remember your preferences
  • Provide a personalized learning experience

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose any information to third parties because we don't collect personal information.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your data is as secure as your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect personal information, there are no privacy concerns for children.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your local data:

  • Reset your progress anytime
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Modify preferences within the app

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with Apple App Store Review Guidelines, GDPR, CCPA, and COPPA.
