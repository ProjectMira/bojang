Privacy Policy for Bojang - Learn Tibetan Language

Effective Date: December 2024

Last Updated: December 2024

Quick Summary: Bojang does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data stays on your device.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Bojang ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application Bojang - Learn Tibetan Language (the "App").

Information We DO NOT Collect

Bojang is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports
  • User-generated content
  • Account information (no login required)
  • Payment information (app is free)
  • Contacts or photos
  • Camera or microphone access

Information We DO Collect

Local Storage Only:

  • Quiz progress and scores (stored locally on your device only)
  • App preferences and settings (stored locally on your device only)
  • Audio preference settings (stored locally on your device only)

Important: This information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal information, we cannot use it. The locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Save your learning progress
  • Remember your preferences
  • Provide a personalized learning experience
  • Track your quiz scores locally

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any information to third parties because we don't collect any personal information.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is as secure as your device
  • We recommend keeping your device updated with the latest security patches
  • Use device lock screens and other security features provided by your device manufacturer

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special privacy concerns for children using our app.

Data Retention

  • Local progress data is retained until you delete the app
  • You can reset your progress at any time through the app settings
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal information, traditional data rights (access, correction, deletion) don't apply. However, you have complete control over your local data:

  • You can reset your progress at any time
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • You can modify preferences within the app

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Since we don't process personal data, no legal basis is required under GDPR.

International Data Transfers

No data is transferred internationally as all data remains on your local device.

Automated Decision Making

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling.
