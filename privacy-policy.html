Privacy Policy for Bojang - Learn Tibetan Language

Effective Date: December 2024

Last Updated: July 12, 2026

Quick Summary: You can use Bojang without an account — in that case all your data stays on your device. If you choose to sign in with Google, we collect your email address, name, and profile picture to create your account and sync your learning progress across devices. We never sell your data, show ads, or track you across other apps.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Bojang ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application Bojang - Learn Tibetan Language (the "App").

Using the App Without an Account

Signing in is optional. If you tap "Skip" on the sign-in screen, the full app is available and the following is stored locally on your device only:

  • Quiz progress and scores
  • App preferences and settings

This information never leaves your device and can be removed at any time by uninstalling the app.

Information We Collect When You Sign In with Google

If you choose to sign in with your Google account, we receive and store the following on our servers:

  • Email address — used to identify your account
  • Name — shown on your profile
  • Profile picture — shown on your profile
  • Learning progress — quiz scores, completed lessons, and streaks, so they can sync across your devices

Google's own handling of your sign-in is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos (other than your Google profile picture), camera, or microphone data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Payment information (the app is free)
  • Analytics or tracking data across other apps and websites

How We Use Information

  • To create and maintain your account (if you sign in)
  • To sync your learning progress across devices
  • To save your preferences and provide a personalized learning experience

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own purposes. Your account data is stored on our backend hosted on Google Cloud, which processes it solely on our behalf.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In — used only to authenticate you when you choose to sign in
  • Google Cloud — hosts our backend and stores account data

Our app does not integrate any third-party advertising or cross-app tracking services.

Data Security

  • All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
  • Local data is as secure as your device — keep it updated and use a lock screen

Children's Privacy

The app's content is suitable for all ages. Children can use the app fully without signing in, in which case no personal information is collected. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13; sign-in requires a Google account, which is subject to Google's own age requirements.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local data is retained until you delete the app
  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below

Your Rights

Depending on where you live (including under GDPR and CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below and we will respond within 30 days.

International Data Transfers

Account data is stored on Google Cloud servers in the United States. If you use the app from outside the United States, your signed-in data will be transferred there.

Automated Decision Making

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and, for material changes, by a notice in the app.
