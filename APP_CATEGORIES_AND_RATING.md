# App Categories and Age Rating Information
## Complete Guide for App Store Connect Configuration

### App Store Categories

**Primary Category:** Education
- **Reason:** Bojang is primarily an educational app focused on language learning
- **Best fit for:** Apps that teach skills, provide educational content, or facilitate learning

**Secondary Category:** Reference
- **Reason:** The app serves as a reference tool for Tibetan language vocabulary and phrases
- **Alternative:** Books (if you want to emphasize the cultural/literary aspect)

### Category Selection Rationale

**Why Education is the Primary Category:**
- Core functionality is teaching Tibetan language
- Structured learning progression (Beginner → Intermediate → Advanced)
- Quiz-based learning methodology
- Progress tracking and educational feedback
- Suitable for students and learners of all ages

**Why Reference is the Secondary Category:**
- App contains comprehensive Tibetan vocabulary
- Serves as a reference for Tibetan script and pronunciation
- Cultural information and context provided
- Can be used for quick lookups and translations

### Alternative Category Options

**If Education is not available or suitable:**
1. **Books** (Primary) - for cultural/literary content
2. **Travel** (Secondary) - for Dharamshala and Tibet-related content
3. **Lifestyle** (Secondary) - for cultural learning and personal development

4. Test different keyword strategies
5. Evaluate competitor category choices

**Performance Indicators:**
- Download rates compared to category averages
- Search ranking for relevant keywords
- User engagement and retention rates
- App Store search visibility
